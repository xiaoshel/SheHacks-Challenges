The Donut Shoppe

Brampton | Rating: 5/5

These are the best donuts in town! They are always freshly baked and taste so delicious. I love picking up a box of dozen assorted donuts to be able to try a variety of flavours. My favourite is the chocolate glazed with sprinkles!

Marco’s Pizzeria

Brampton | Rating: 2.5/5

If you came to Marco’s for the atmosphere and the service, you went to the wrong place. I went to the Pizzeria at around dinner time and had the worst time trying to get the attention of our server. The place was loud and busy, which makes it not a great place for any conversation. However, the food was good and the value for the money paid is evident. All in all, I would say this place is great for people who want good food for a low price, but be warned that service and atmosphere is not the best.

Niuda Noodles

Markham | Rating: 4/5

Dan Dan Noodles — Their classic hand-pulled noodles with minced pork and house specialty chili sauce and sprinkled with pixie dust.Excellent serving size, worth it for your money. Great flavour, although wait times were long. It just has that je ne sais quoi— Ingredients tasted fresh and fed the soul.
